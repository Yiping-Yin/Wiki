# Product History

Loom began as a personal thinking tool and matured into a personal knowledge
identity platform: a portfolio people can inspect, a knowledge base people can
trust, and a personal AI people can talk to.

The name points to the structure. Sources are the warp: stable evidence that
keeps the work under tension. Notes, drafts, AI conversations, and projects are
the weft: the crosswise passes that turn sources into identity, judgment, and
output.

Yiping's Loom is the first reference instance. About, UNSW, Quantnet, WQU, and
Claude show the first real shelves, but the product is the repeatable model
behind those shelves.

## Product Timeline

- **2026-04-13** — Loom began as a personal thinking tool for turning scattered
  material into a readable working surface.
- **2026-04-15** — The product moved toward source-bound shelves so learning,
  projects, and drafts could share one structure.
- **2026-05-02** — The first real shelves made the portfolio-with-proof model
  concrete across study, work, and AI collaboration.
- **2026-06-01** — The mature narrative names Loom as a future path toward a
  platform for everyone, not only one user's archive.

## Three-Layer Product Narrative

Loom should not choose between portfolio, source-backed identity, and personal
AI. The mature story is all three:

- **Portfolio with proof** — people first understand Loom as an inspectable
  homepage for work, learning paths, certificates, projects, writing, and
  process.
- **Source to identity** — Loom is different because the portfolio is backed by
  sources, notes, drafts, decisions, and history.
- **AI persona** — once the archive exists, a personal AI can answer from the
  user's actual profile, source archive, portfolio evidence, and process
  records.

Yiping's Loom is the first reference instance, not the product boundary.

## Positioning Stack

- **Personal knowledge postcard** — a compact summary of the person, their
  shelves, and the proof behind their direction.
- **Portfolio site** — a public surface where work, learning, and projects are
  presented with inspectable backing.
- **Knowledge base** — a private and public source layer that keeps context,
  provenance, and decisions connected.
- **Virtual personal AI** — a conversational expression of the same source-backed
  identity.

## Reusable Pitch Copy

Loom is a personal knowledge identity platform: a portfolio people can inspect,
a knowledge base people can trust, and a personal AI people can talk to.
