# Welcome to your local knowledge corpus

This is a sample document. Delete `knowledge/sample/` and drop your own `.md`, `.mdx`, or `.txt` files anywhere under `knowledge/` — they will appear on the atlas after the next `npx tsx scripts/build-atlas.ts`.

Knowledge can include: paper notes, meeting minutes, book chapters, research journals, raw arXiv-vanity HTML scraped to markdown, Obsidian vault dumps, anything text.

The pipeline is **fully local** — embeddings run on your CPU via ONNX, no API keys required.
